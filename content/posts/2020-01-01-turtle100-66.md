<h2>题目：绘制下面的图形</h2>
<figure class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="max-width: 432px"><a href="/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/2020010206561774.png"><img class="wp-image-806 size-full" src="/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/2020010206561774.png" alt="python turtle 100-66" width="432" height="312" /></a><figcaption>Python 海龟绘图 100 题——第 66 题</figcaption></figure>
<h2>解析：</h2>
绘制五角星，每个角有一个小圆。
<h2>答案：</h2>
<strong>方法一 使用循环绘制圆</strong>
<pre class="prettyprint linenums">import turtle as t
for i in range(0,4):
    t.fd(100)
    t.lt(90)
    for j in range(0,180):
        t.fd(0.0174*50)
        t.lt(1)
    t.lt(90)
    t.fd(100)
    t.lt(90)</pre>
<strong>方法二 使用 circle 方法绘制圆</strong>
<pre class="prettyprint linenums">import turtle as t
for i in range(0,4):
    t.fd(100)
    t.lt(90)
    t.circle(50, 180)
    t.lt(90)
    t.fd(100)
    t.lt(90)</pre>
