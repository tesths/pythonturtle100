<h2>题目：绘制下面的图形</h2>
<figure class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="max-width: 432px"><a href="/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/2020041808122811.png"><img class="wp-image-960 size-full" src="/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/2020041808122811.png" alt="python turtle 100-90" width="432" height="312" /></a><figcaption>Python 海龟绘图 100 题——第 90 题</figcaption></figure>
<h2>解析：</h2>
每个正方形的边长依次减少 20。此题与 <a href="/turtle100-70/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">第 70 题</a> 为同题不同解。
<h2>答案：</h2>
<pre class="prettyprint linenums">import turtle as t
def zfx(d):
    for i in range(0, 4):
        t.fd(d)
        t.lt(90)
    t.pu()
    t.fd(10)
    t.lt(90)
    t.fd(10)
    t.rt(90)
    t.pd()

s = 120
for i in range(0, 5):
    zfx(s)
    s = s - 20</pre>
