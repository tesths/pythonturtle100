<h2>题目：绘制下面的图形</h2>
<figure class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="max-width: 432px"><a href="/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/2019120206564265.png"><img class="wp-image-749 size-full" src="/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/2019120206564265.png" alt="python turtle 100-57" width="432" height="312" /></a><figcaption>Python 海龟绘图 100 题——第 57 题</figcaption></figure>
<h2>解析：</h2>
使用循环的嵌套绘制八个正方形的图形。
<h2>答案：</h2>
<pre class="prettyprint linenums">import turtle as t
for i in range(0,4):
    for j in range(0,2):
        t.fd(50)
        t.rt(90)
    t.lt(90)
    for j in range(0,4):
        t.fd(50)
        t.lt(90)
    t.rt(90)
    for j in range(0,2):
        t.fd(50)
        t.rt(90)
    t.rt(90)</pre>
