<h2>题目：绘制下面的图形</h2>
<figure class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="max-width: 337px"><a href="/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/2019121108174679.jpg"><img class="wp-image-780 size-full" src="/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/2019121108174679.jpg" alt="python turtle 100-62" width="337" height="215" /></a><figcaption>Python 海龟绘图 100 题——第 62 题</figcaption></figure>
<h2>解析：</h2>
绘制大圆，外切四个均匀分布的小圆。
<h2>答案：</h2>
<strong>方法一 使用循环绘制圆</strong>
<pre class="prettyprint linenums">import turtle as t
for i in range(0,4):
    for j in range(0,90):
        t.fd(0.0174*50)
        t.lt(1)
    for j in range(0,360):
        t.fd(0.0174*10)
        t.rt(1)</pre>
<strong>方法二 使用 circle 方法绘制圆</strong>
<pre class="prettyprint linenums">import turtle as t
for i in range(0,4):
    t.circle(50,90)
    t.lt(180)
    t.circle(10)
    t.lt(180)</pre>
