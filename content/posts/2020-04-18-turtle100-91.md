<h2>题目：绘制下面的图形</h2>
<figure class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="max-width: 432px"><a href="/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/2020041808180484.png"><img class="wp-image-963 size-full" src="/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/2020041808180484.png" alt="python turtle 100-91" width="432" height="312" /></a><figcaption>Python 海龟绘图 100 题——第 91 题</figcaption></figure>
<h2>解析：</h2>
四个并列的正方形，每个正方形的边长是上一个的一半。
<h2>答案：</h2>
<pre class="prettyprint linenums">import turtle as t
def zfx(d):
    for i in range(0, 4):
        t.fd(d)
        t.lt(90)
    t.fd(d)

s = 120
for i in range(0, 4):
    zfx(s)
    s = s / 2</pre>
