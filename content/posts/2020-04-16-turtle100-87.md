<h2>解析：</h2>
绘制三个圆弧组成的图形。
<h2>答案：</h2>
方法一
<pre class="prettyprint linenums">import turtle as t
def banyuan(r):
    for i in range(0, 180):
        t.fd(0.0174*r)
        t.rt(1)
    return

t.lt(90)
for i in range(0, 3):
    banyuan(50)
    t.rt(60)</pre>
方法二
<pre class="prettyprint linenums">import turtle as t
t.lt(90)
for i in range(0, 3):
    t.circle(-50, 180)
    t.rt(60)</pre>
