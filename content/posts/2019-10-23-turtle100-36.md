<h2>题目：绘制下面的图形</h2>
<figure class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="max-width: 432px"><a href="/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/2019102401443710.png"><img class="wp-image-426 size-full" src="/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/2019102401443710.png" alt="python turtle 100-36" width="432" height="312" /></a><figcaption>Python 海龟绘图 100 题——第 36 题</figcaption></figure>
<h2>解析：</h2>
使用二重循环绘制四个方块。
<h2>答案：</h2>
方法一
<pre class="prettyprint linenums">import turtle as t
for i in range(0,4):
    for j in range(0,4):
        t.fd(50)
        t.rt(90)
    t.rt(90)
    t.fd(50)</pre>
方法二
<pre class="prettyprint linenums">import turtle as t
for i in range(0,4):
    for j in range(0,7):
        t.fd(50)
        t.rt(90)
    t.rt(180)</pre>
