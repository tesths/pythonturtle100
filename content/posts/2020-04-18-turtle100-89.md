<h2>解析：</h2>
绘制长度依次增加的正方形。此题与 <a href="/turtle100-68/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">第 68 题</a> 为同题不同解
<h2>答案：</h2>
<pre class="prettyprint linenums">import turtle as t
def bian(d):
    t.fd(d)
    t.rt(90)

s = 20
t.lt(90)
for i in range(0, 12):
    bian(s)
    s = s + 10</pre>
