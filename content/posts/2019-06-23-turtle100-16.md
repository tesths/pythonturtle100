<h2>题目：绘制下面的图形</h2>
<figure class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="max-width: 360px"><a href="/wp-content/uploads/2019/06/2019062903263535.png"><img class="wp-image-218 size-full" src="/wp-content/uploads/2019/06/2019062903263535.png" alt="python turtle 100-16" width="360" height="260" /></a><figcaption>Python 海龟绘图 100 题——第 16 题</figcaption></figure>
<h2>解析：</h2>
综合应用，绘制有对角线的正方形。
<h2>答案：</h2>
不用循环
<pre class="prettyprint linenums">import turtle as t
t.fd(100)
t.rt(90)
t.fd(100)
t.home()
t.rt(90)
t.fd(100)
t.undo()
t.lt(90)
t.fd(100)</pre>
使用循环
<pre class="prettyprint linenums">import turtle as t
for i in range(0,6):
    t.fd(100)
    t.rt(90)
t.home()</pre>
