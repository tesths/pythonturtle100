<h2>题目：绘制下面的图形</h2>
<figure class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="max-width: 432px"><a href="/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/2019101609171218.png"><img class="wp-image-374 size-full" src="/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/2019101609171218.png" alt="python turtle 100-28" width="432" height="312" /></a><figcaption>Python 海龟绘图 100 题——第 28 题</figcaption></figure>
<h2>解析：</h2>
循环的嵌套，绘制连续五个三角形。
<h2>答案：</h2>
方法一
<pre class="prettyprint linenums">import turtle as t
for i in range(0,5):
    for j in range(0,3):
        t.fd(50)
        t.lt(120)
    t.fd(50)</pre>
方法二
<pre class="prettyprint linenums">import turtle as t
for i in range(0,5):
    for j in range(0,4):
        t.fd(50)
        t.lt(120)
    t.rt(120)</pre>
