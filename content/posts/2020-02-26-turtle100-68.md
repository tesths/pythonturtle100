<h2>题目：绘制下面的图形</h2>
<figure class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="max-width: 432px"><a href="/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/2020022706394455.png"><img class="wp-image-838 size-full" src="/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/2020022706394455.png" alt="python turtle 100-68" width="432" height="312" /></a><figcaption>Python 海龟绘图 100 题——第 68 题</figcaption></figure>
<h2>解析：</h2>
绘制长度依次增加的正方形。
<h2>答案：</h2>
<pre class="prettyprint linenums">import turtle as t
s = 10
for i in range(0, 13):
    t.fd(s)
    t.rt(90)
    s = s + 10</pre>
