<h2>题目：绘制下面的图形</h2>
<figure class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="max-width: 432px"><a href="/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/2020022708520660.png"><img class="wp-image-849 size-full" src="/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/2020022708520660.png" alt="python turtle 100-70" width="432" height="312" /></a><figcaption>Python 海龟绘图 100 题——第 70 题</figcaption></figure>
<h2>解析：</h2>
每个正方形的边长依次增加 20。
<h2>答案：</h2>
<pre class="prettyprint linenums">import turtle as t
s = 40
for i in range(0, 5):
    for j in range(0, 4):
        t.fd(s)
        t.lt(90)
    t.pu()
    t.bk(10)
    t.lt(90)
    t.bk(10)
    t.rt(90)
    t.pd()
    s=s+20</pre>
