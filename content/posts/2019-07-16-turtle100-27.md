<h2>解析：</h2>
循环的嵌套，绘制田字。
<h2>答案：</h2>
<pre class="prettyprint linenums">import turtle as t
for i in range(0,4):
    for j in range(0,4):
        t.fd(100)
        t.rt(90)
    t.rt(90)</pre>
